Account Deletion Request – Kado Booking

Thank you for using Kado Booking.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email: stellashco@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Send the request using the Google account used to sign in.

Data Deleted

  • Account information (email address)
  • Authentication data
  • User-related app data

Data Retention

All data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days. Some data may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes and removed within 30 days.

Important

Deleting your app account does not delete your Google account.
